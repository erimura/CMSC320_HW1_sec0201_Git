Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: As a version control system, it allows you to track modifications and switch to different code versions if there are issues. Also allows you to collaborate with people.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to the biomedical research field. In this field,
there are many large, complex datasets from gene databases to clinical trial data
that needs to be cleaned, interpreted, and used to create insights that drive innovation.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging preserves the entire history of the repository while rebasing rewrites
the history to create a linear history without branching.
